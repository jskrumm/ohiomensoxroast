Richard Mansfield is the Senior Pastor of the New Beginnings Church of God in Albuquerque, New Mexico 
where he has served from July 1982 to December 1995 and from July 1999 to the present. 
A vibrant and much sought-after speaker across the nation, he has provided leadership for a number of 
Church of God churches and church organizations throughout the southwestern US, as well as national leadership 
to a variety of ministries, including Concilio, the National Board of Christian Education, 
and the International Convention of the Church of God. A pastor, television speaker, chaplain, and drug counselor, 
Richard comes with a wealth of experience and stories to encourage and challenge.
